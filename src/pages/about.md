# Seungwon Yang

양승원 [jaŋ sɯŋwʌn]

<img src="/img/seungwon.jpg" alt="Seungwon Yang" class="about-profile-image" />

Seungwon Yang is a Ph.D. student at [HISLab](https://his-lab.org), [POSTECH](https://postech.ac.kr). His research broadly lies in **Human-Computer Interaction (HCI)**, with a focus on designing interactive systems that empower diverse users. Specifically, his interests span **accessibility**, **gamification**, **AR/VR**, and **location-based games**.

He is also interested in the evolving role of humans in the age of AI — exploring how people can remain active, creative agents as intelligent systems become more prevalent.
